Raita Recipe

An Indian dish best served as a sauce with Polao or Biryani

Ingredients

  • 250g Greek yogurt
  • 1 garlic clove
  • thumb-sized piece ginger, finely grated
  • ½ small pack coriander, chopped
  • 0.25 small pack mint, leaves chopped juice 0.5 lime
  • ½ tsp garam masala, plus a little extra to serve
  • ¼ cucumber, grated and lightly squeezed

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients and season.
  2. Serve sprinkled with extra garam masala.
